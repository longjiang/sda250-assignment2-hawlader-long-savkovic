What a very surprising let down. I am a fan of DJ Muggs, and the rest of the artists on this album, but for the amount of starpower compiled onto one CD one would expect better. The very poor beat mixing and sloppy freestyling has me at a loss. With an exeption of one or maybe two songs, this CD is not worth a second look. Maybe listen to your buddies copy, to check it out, if your curious
